Memorable Quotes

It’s hard to believe that the hysterical FX series “It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia” has been on the air for 12 seasons, with production of season thirteen underway. Series co-writer, producer and star Glenn Howerton (Dennis Reynolds) recently tweeted that the new season is “some of the funniest stuff [they’ve] ever done.” So in anticipation for the upcoming season, set to air in 2018, I've gathered up a few of the best catchphrases and one-liners from the gang.
